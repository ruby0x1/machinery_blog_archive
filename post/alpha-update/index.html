Alpha Update

Dec 19, 2019

Just a short note to let you know that we just released a new update of our alpha release. In addition to numerous fixes and UI improvements, this release comes with three new big systems:

  • Creation Graphs is our flexible system for importing and processing resources from DCC assets for use in the engine.

  • The engine now has Physics simulation support, provided by PhysX.

  • We’ve also added support for Animation, including Animation State Machines.

Our alpha release is closed at the moment, but if you’re interested in checking it out, send us a mail at [email protected] and we’ll send you an invite.

Best,

by Tricia, Niklas, and Tobias
